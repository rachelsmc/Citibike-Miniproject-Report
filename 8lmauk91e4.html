Methodology
The test we chose to use is the one-tailed t-test because we are comparing the means of two samples of different sizes - trip duration of females and trip duration of males. This is in-line with both reviewers' suggestion of using the t-test. Minor tweaks from the original proposal (including the whole year instead of just the month of January) was also added so th . Specifically, we looked at Welch's t-test which does not assume equal population variance.  On python, we used the scipy package to run a one-tailed t-test.  For our one-tailed t-test, if the t-statistic is positive and the p-value divided by two is less than \(\alpha\), we can reject the null hypothesis.

Methodology
The test we chose to use is the one-tailed t-test because we are comparing the means of two samples of different sizes - trip duration of females and trip duration of males. Specifically, we looked at Welch's t-test which does not assume equal population variance.

The two sample Z test is a good test for comparing two means as well . Although the ANOVA is not a test for comparison of means, it will work for two or more independent variables which we have in this case.
